Indonesia

Indonesia is a great place to loose yourself on a beach

My time in Indonesia was spent travelling from Jakarta to Bali. Here's my top 5 things to do:

  1. Gili Islands: We spent 5 days on Gili T but also did a day trip to Gili Air
  2. Mount Bromo: Take a trip to the view point in a jeep before climbing the crater
  3. Sky Bar in Jakarta: Get city wide views of the Indonesian capital
  4. See the Elephants in Ubud: If you can, go early morning and have breakfast with the elephants - You get to wash them and ride them bare back
  5. Monkey Forest in Ubud: Run around with monkeys - just be aware if you have food they will try eat it!
